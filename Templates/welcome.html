About CampusSync

CampusSync is a Smart Classroom and Timetable Scheduler platform designed to simplify academic management for educational institutions. It provides centralized management of students, teachers, timetables, attendance, subjects, classrooms, and reports. The platform improves efficiency, reduces scheduling conflicts, and enhances communication across the campus ecosystem.
